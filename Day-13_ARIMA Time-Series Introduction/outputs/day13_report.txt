GPU LSTM 621.79 725.62     48.89

----------------------------------------------------------------------
KEY TAKEAWAYS
----------------------------------------------------------------------

1. Time-series data MUST be split temporally — never random shuffle
2. ARIMA(p,d,q): p=AR lags, d=differencing, q=MA errors
3. Stationarity is required for ARIMA — differencing (d) fixes trends
4. Weekly seasonality in COVID data (fewer weekend reports)
5. LSTM captures complex nonlinear temporal patterns
6. Exponential Smoothing handles trend + seasonality explicitly
7. MAPE is the most intuitive metric for stakeholders
8. Forecast error grows with horizon — near-term is always more accurate
